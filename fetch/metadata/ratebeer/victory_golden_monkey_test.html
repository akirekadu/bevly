COMMERCIAL DESCRIPTION
Enchanting and enlightening, this golden, frothy ale boasts an intriguing herbal aroma, warming alcohol esters on the tongue and light, but firm body to finish. Exotic spices add subtle notes to both the aroma and flavor. Strong, sensual and satisfying.

The aroma is warm, inviting, and outstanding. Banana and clove action right off the bat but not as much as in a Hefeweizen. More citrus than herbal or floral and that’s just great. The appearance is flawless, featuring a perfectly gold color with a puffy white head that forms lacing all by itself. Constant bubbles shoot upwards. Medium bitter medium sweet and the alcohol is well-noticed here. Flavor is very heavy on the clove at the front of the tongue. Yes, cloves, and a dash of cinnamon is what I detect. The aftertaste is where this one falls a little short, as I feel like I’ve had better tripels before. Still, all in all, an outstanding beer in this class and has a nice warming effect on me.
