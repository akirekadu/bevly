House Ales

  • “The Black Code”

    Black Ale A hopped up black ale brewed in tribute to the mighty Wo Fat. Spinal tap may have said "none more black" but they hadn't tried The Black Code ...brewed with massive amounts of Midnight (roasted) wheat so expect big roast flavors (coffee, licorice). Bittered with Southern Cross & Chinook, finished and dry hopped with Columbus & Mount Hood for a big hop presence.  6.5%

  • The Sentinel

    Smoked Red Ale Brewed with our good friend Will Cook. Born from our mutual love of Judas priest this, the next in our “Smoke em if you got ‘em” series, is an English pale ale style ale brewed from a base of English pale malt with crystal and roasted malts & Torrified wheat. Briess(American)and Best(German) smoked malts (15% of the malt grist)provided the smoke. Bittered with English Challenger and kent Goldings, finished with English Bramling Cross. 7.7%

  • King of the Knight Time World

    Black Rye Ale Brewed with notes of spice (rye), licorice, and a mellow roast bitterness. 8.4%

  • Jacobs Summer Celebration

    Summer Ale As always the brew is based around the little man himself w/ 8.8lb of bittering hops and 8.8lb of finishing/dry hops split evenly between American and English varieties (to represent his parentage of course). Needless to say, because Jacob was born on the 4th, the dominant American finishing hop is a variety called Liberty! The malt base is English & Canadian, the yeast strain American (courtesy of our friends at DuClaw). (12oz snifter) 9.2%

  • Bulldog Bitter

    English Bitter - This classic English amber bitter pairs beautifully with a World class game of footie. We'll be running it as a special during each game while supplies last. Brewing specs: malt grist: Halcyon Pale Ale malt, wheat malt, crystal 45 and a splash of chocolate malt…bittering hops: Challenger & Bramling Cross, and finishing hops: Kent Goldings & Bramling Cross. 4.3%

  • Sea of Spears

    Red Wheat Ale Red Wheat Ale Bittered with Centennial & Citra and finished & dry hopped with lots of Citra & Nugget for a big hop presence. 6.8%

  • Winter’s Wolves

    Dark Ale A tribute to our favorite band, The Sword … this is Heavy Metal in liquid form … a hop forward dark ale brewed with a little honey and Scottish heather and aged on American Oak. 7.4%

  • Sea of Spears (Nitro)

    Red Wheat Ale  Red Wheat Ale Bittered with Centennial & Citra and finished & dry hopped with lots of Citra & Nugget for a big hop presence. 6.8%

  • Draft Punk

    American IPA  An Englishman’s interpretation of an American IPA. Not as over-the-top as some of its “true” American counterparts, but with more hops than we typically use.  

  • Bishops Breakfast (Nitro)

    Oatmeal Stout  Dark malt flavors, just a touch of malt sourness and smooth silky mouthfeel combine in perfect harmony. 4.4%

  • 3 Lions Ale

    Strong Brown Ale / A strong English brown ale, full-bodied with underlying hints of caramel sweetness. 7.5%
