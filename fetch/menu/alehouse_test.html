Wines

Red

  • Zen Of Zin - Red Zinfandel

    The rich, luscious mouthfeel, deep garnet color, and lofting aromas can be traced back to the enlightening blend of Zinfandel, Petite Sirah, Syrah, and Carignane.

  • Dreaming Tree - Crush (Red Blend)

    A blend of the North Coast’s finest varieties, this wine pulls you in with a smoky berry note and then jumps up with a raspberry jam. Blend of 78% Merlot, 13% Syrah, 6% Zinfandel, 2% Petite Sirah, 1% Malbec, 1% Cabernet Sauvignon.

  • La Playa - Cabernet

    Rich maroon color. One the nose, ripe strawberry with hints of black pepper. It has great balance between structure and flavor with a light approachable palate

  • La Playa - Carménère

    Velvety light purple. On the nose, ripe blueberries with a hint of fine smoke. It has good ripe fruit presence with rounded tannins.

  • Don Rodolfo - Pinot Noir

     An appealing red garnet color. On the nose, medium intensity, very fruity, and black cherry aromas. This is a young, medium structured Pinot with a savory

  • Don Rodolfo - Malbec

    An intense ruby red color with violet flashes. Aromas of berries, cassis and plums. This Malbec has rich deep flavors, with smooth and round tannins.

  • Avia - Cabernet

    This wine is a dark ruby red color with a light shade of pomegranate. An intense nose of blackberry, hints of green pepper and aroma of the toasted oak barrels marry nicely for a very good overall impression. This wine has depth and rich full body flavors with spice and cocoa and forward fruity notes.

  • Avia - Merlot

    The color of this wine is a dark ruby red with slight shades of violet. An intense, complex nose with bright scents of ripened cherries, figs, strawberries and oak result in a taste that has lovely ripe fruit and full body flavors. Light acid with nice depth of flavors. Good varietal characters are noted and oak barrel maturing result in a harmonious rounding of flavors. A winner from Chile's Central Valley.

  • Avia - Pinot Noir

    The wine is a pleasing ruby red color. A bright flowery nose with hints of strawberries. The taste of this wine is distinctly fruity, lightly acidic with a nice harmony of characteristics. The wine is medium bodied with a light finish in Burgundian style.

White

  • J. Lohr - Riesling

    Enticing aromas of apple, apricot, peach, Bosc pear, and rose petal.  The palate echoes the fruit aromas with a combination of refreshing acidity.

  • Simply Naked - Moscato

    This un-oaked Moscato is sweetly balanced with floral notes and flavors of orange marmalade, peach, and honey.

  • Ruffino Lumina - Pinot Grigio

    Pinot Grigio Straw yellow in color. Intense floral notes are followed by aromas of citrus and pear. Refined notes of minerality are balanced with hints of pineapple and sage, making Lumina a medium-bodied wine with a lean and lasting finish

  • La Playa - Un-Oaked Chardonnay

    A rich gold hay color. On the nose, aromas of peach and apricot. It is a crisp refreshing wine with lots of bright green apple.

  • Fritz De Katz - Riesling

    This wine is produced in one of the best growing regions in Germany. The Mosel region is also famous for its top-notch Riesling which is luscious and fruity with pleasant sweetness and crisp acidity.

  • La Playa - Sauvignon Blanc

    A delicate pale gold. On the nose, aromas of lemon, fresh pineapple with hints of lavender. It is a bright well balanced wine with a clean minerality on the finish.

  • Avia - Pinot Grigio

    An easy drinking Pinot Grigio. The bouquet has the subtle aroma of mature pear and apricot blossom. This wine is crisp and friendly on the palate with medium body, medium dryness and a clean light finish.

  • Avia - Chardonnay

    The pleasing flowery aroma reminds us of apples and peach, rounded nicely with the scent of vanilla and hazelnut. The taste of the wine is fruity, with good acidity and crispness. A pleasant oaky aroma is evident. The residual sugar harmonizes well for a pleasant finish.
